
This project focuses on analyzing human resources data for 500 employees. My main goal was to visualize workforce distribution, experience levels, and demographic trends to support HR decision-making.

What I Analyzed:
Demographics: Visualized the age and gender distribution across the company.

Experience Tracking: Analyzed years of experience to identify talent clusters.

Geographic Distribution: Mapped out where employees are located to understand logistical trends.

Tools Used:
Power BI for the interactive dashboard.

Excel for initial data cleaning and preprocessing.

Challenges:
Working with a dataset of 500 employees required careful data cleaning to ensure that the geographic and experience data were consistent before building the final dashboard.
